# Social Systems

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Goals
- Make belief, trust, and power legible and consequential.
- Provide non-combat challenges and “politics as gameplay”.

## Relationship graph
- Directed edges with dimensions:
  - Trust
  - Affection
  - Fear
  - Respect
  - Debt
- Group identities:
  - households
  - guilds
  - cults
  - pantheon-aligned factions

## Social mechanics
- Councils and votes
- Rumor propagation (driven by trust graph)
- Cult formation (belief divergence over time)
- Scapegoating during crises
- Hero worship / martyrdom for fallen champions

## Interaction points (Champion)
- Speech acts (reassure, command, confess, accuse)
- Trials (assign blame, restore trust)
- Ritual leadership (creates shared meaning)

## Interaction points (Deity)
- Signs that target factions
- Blessings that create patronage networks
- “Doubt” seeds (if you play antagonistic deity)

## Failure modes (good for drama)
- Miracle debunked → schism
- Scarcity → scapegoats
- Rival god propaganda → civil conflict
