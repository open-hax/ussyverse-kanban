# Progression, Prestige, and Retirement

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Core idea
Champions are mortal. Their lives become **myths**. The deity’s power is shaped by those myths.

## Champion arc
- Growth through deeds, relationships, and relic recovery
- Retirement/death triggers:
  - Legacy summary
  - transfer of artifacts
  - social reshuffle
  - deity standing change

## Prestige (meta-progression)
- Earned from:
  - verified miracles linked to your era
  - settlement milestones (survived winters, sealed gates)
  - mythic choices (sacrifice, defiance, mercy)
- Spent on:
  - starting boons for next champion
  - unlocking miracle archetypes
  - negotiating pantheon constraints

## Legacy mechanics
- Buildings and institutions persist (council hall, temple).
- Stories persist as “Witness Logs” and affect belief.
- Some legacies become taboos or laws.

## Risk
Too much prestige can attract pantheon envy or Chaos interest.
