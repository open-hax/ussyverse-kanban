# Audio Direction

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Target vibe
Experimental electronic / dark synth / glitch ambient / industrial sci‑fi:
- 90–105 BPM, half-time feel
- heavy sub-bass, fractured beats
- distorted modular synths, granular noise
- reversed choirs, glitch stutters, radio static, corrupted tape
- low drones
- mood: uncanny → beautiful → destabilizing

## Structural approach
- Slow burn → collapse → strange calm
- Avoid traditional verse/chorus dominance in ambient layers; treat the world as a failing system *musically*.

## System-driven music hooks
- **Chaos pressure** increases noise layer density.
- **Verification success** triggers harmonic “resolution” stingers.
- **Pantheon conflict** introduces rival motifs.

## Vocal approach (optional)
- Minimal: whispered phrases, chopped samples, pitch-shifted murmurs.
- Bilingual fragments (JP/EN) possible for thematic flare.

## Required implementation feature
- Music must be parameterized (stems) to respond to world state:
  - thinness
  - entropy debt
  - devotion
  - threat level
  - faction tension
