# Design Pillars & Non-Goals

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Pillars
1. **Duality with continuity**
   - Champion and Deity are not separate games; they are two lenses on one world state.

2. **Mythic logic, system consequences**
   - Miracles are powerful, but the world responds with belief, fear, envy, and adaptation.

3. **Emergence over scripts**
   - The best stories happen when systems collide: needs, politics, chaos leakage, and player choices.

4. **Readable complexity**
   - Deep systems, but with strong visualization: cause → effect should be inferable.

5. **Glitch as aesthetic and mechanic**
   - “Corruption” is not just visual flair; it is a game-state force with rules.

## Non-goals (for now)
- Pure RTS micro or pure action game; combat exists, but is in service to the sim.
- MMO-scale persistence; keep “world stories” personal and legible.
- Fully procedural everything; curated mythic beats matter.

## Quality bars
- Every miracle must be:
  - **Tempting**
  - **Costed**
  - **Visible**
  - **Interpretable**
  - **Consequential**
