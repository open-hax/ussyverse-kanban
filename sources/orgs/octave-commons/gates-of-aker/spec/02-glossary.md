# Glossary

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

- **Gates of Aker (The Gates):** A boundary/threshold where reality interfaces with Primordial Chaos.
- **Primordial Chaos:** A destabilizing cosmological force; manifests as corruption, anomalies, and “black butterflies”.
- **Black Butterflies:** Visual + mechanical representation of Chaos shards; can “land on hearts” and alter agents.
- **Champion:** The embodied player role; acts directly in the world during the “Day”.
- **Deity:** The influencing player role; acts indirectly via miracles during the “Night”.
- **Pantheon:** The set of gods / powerful entities and their relationships, rules, and politics.
- **Miracle:** A rule-bending intervention initiated by a deity (player or NPC) with cost and fallout.
- **Verification:** The world’s process for deciding whether a miracle “counts” (belief, witnesses, evidence, counterclaims).
- **Prestige:** Meta-progression/legacy accumulated across champion lives and deity eras.
- **Witness Log:** A canonical record of significant events used for verification, storytelling, and replay.
- **Promethean Error Loop:** A thematic motif: the world improves through theft of “fire” but repeats failure patterns.
