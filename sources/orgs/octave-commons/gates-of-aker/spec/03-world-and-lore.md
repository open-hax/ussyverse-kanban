# World & Lore

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Tone
Mythic Greek structure reinterpreted as **industrial sci‑fi / glitch fantasy**:
- Ancient archetypes (Prometheus, Pandora) expressed as system operators and cosmic protocols.
- Beauty and dread coexist: uncanny, destabilizing, then strange calm.

## Core myth beats (canon)
1. **Prometheus steals fire** from the Duck‑Space Olympus glitch gods.
2. Fire is given to **mud‑born duck people**, making them “people people” (self-reflective agents).
3. From Pandora’s Box (or its functional equivalent), **Primordial Chaos** erupts as **black butterflies**:
   - Each butterfly has a demonic red/purple glitch glow.
   - A single enormous white light appears as a nodal opposite on each side of the Gates.
4. A butterfly lands on each heart as “the gift of flame” enters the mind’s eye:
   - This links consciousness, agency, and corruption risk.
5. Prometheus is punished (cost of gifting agency).

## Setting primitives
- **Space-future ancient Greece:** marble + neon, columns + antennae, temples as servers.
- **Glitch Gods:** pantheon entities with constraints, rivalries, and limited jurisdiction.
- **The Gates:** places where reality is thin; both physical sites and metaphysical states.

## Narrative function of Chaos
Chaos is not “evil” in a simple sense; it is:
- entropy,
- novelty,
- mutation,
- misalignment,
- and a mirror of the system’s hidden assumptions.

## Factions (initial)
- **Settlement / Duck People:** emergent society with needs, norms, and internal politics.
- **Pantheon:** competing cosmic stakeholders.
- **Chaos Forms:** anomalies, rifts, corrupted agents, invasive patterns.
- **Rival Mortals/Creatures:** e.g., “space cats” thread as a hostile or complex counterpart faction.

## Lore affordances for gameplay
- Every systemic mechanic should have a mythic “skin”:
  - production chain → “ritual chain”
  - research → “interpretation of omens”
  - tech tree → “recovered fire”
