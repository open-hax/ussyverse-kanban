# Pantheon & Gods

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Pantheon structure
- Pantheon entities are both characters and rule modules.
- Each god has:
  - Domains (what they control)
  - Constraints (what they cannot do)
  - Needs (what they seek)
  - Relationship edges (allies/rivals)
  - Preferred narrative “proofs” (what convinces their followers)

## Player relationship to pantheon
- The player as Deity is either:
  - an emergent god,
  - a promoted champion,
  - or a “stolen fire” anomaly tolerated by rules.

## Interdiction mechanic
Gods can place **Interdicts**:
- temporarily blocks a miracle type in a region
- or adds extra cost/verification burden
Interdicts are negotiated, broken, or outmaneuvered.

- Memory/Witness

## Pantheon events
- Councils: periodic shifts in rules
- Challenges: gods wager constraints on outcomes
- Schisms: factions split, creating new mechanics
