# Economy & Resources

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Goals
- Make resources feel physical and story-connected.
- Avoid excessive micromanagement; emphasize bottlenecks and tradeoffs.

## Resource classes
- **Sustenance:** food, water, shelter
- **Construction:** mud/stone/metal composites
- **Ritual:** incense, relic dust, sigils
- **Authority:** decrees, council mandates
- **Cosmic:** devotion, influence, entropy debt

## Market dynamics (optional, later)
- Barter and obligations rather than gold.
- Patronage and temple tithe systems.

## Scarcity as narrative driver
- Scarcity increases fear → affects verification and cult formation.
- Abundance from miracles shifts norms and expectations.
