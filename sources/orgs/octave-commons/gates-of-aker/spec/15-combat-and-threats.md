# Combat & Threats

This document was created with the assistance of an AI.

## Design position
Combat exists to express stakes and reveal system interactions, not to become the whole game.

## Threat taxonomy
1. **Rift events** (Chaos): anomalies, warped creatures, reality tears
2. **Faction raids** (mortal): rivals, opportunists
3. **Ideological conflict** (social): riots, cult clashes
4. **Environmental disasters**: glitch storms, corrupted harvests

## “Tide of battle” moments
Occasionally the world is on the brink:
- multiple failures align
- the settlement is about to collapse
- then an extreme intervention occurs (miracle, relic, or “pickles” style wildcards)

## Wildcard entities (from the conversation thread)
- “Pickle” shock troops / bizarre constructs that change the tide.
  - Spec as optional rare “construct archetype” created by extreme research/chaos.

## Champion combat model (lightweight)
- Tactical pause optional
- Roles (tank/support/ranged) emergent from gear and traits
- Injuries persist and affect social status

## Deity combat influence
- Area blessings/wards
- Probability bends (misses, criticals) with verification risk
- Rift sealing as high-cost miracle
